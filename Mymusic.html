My Music

Music is a way for me to cope with the various events going on around me. Wether it be me studying for a test, in the car with my friends, or just sitting at home thinking about life. Music is a gateway to your emotions and different genres or songs in general can touch each spot.

Most people listen to one type of genre of music, but I like to listen to all kinds of genres. For example, when my friends and I are in the car, we bump to hip hop/ rap songs, but say I'm just chilling at home, I like more mellow rock songs or even pop. Below is a list of various artists/bands I like to listen to:

  • Drake (Hip Hop/ Rap)
  • The Fray (Rock)
  • The Red Hot Chilli Peppers (Rock)
  • Lil Uzi Vert (Rap)

I decided to put this on my website because music is something that will always be with me and as I get older, the music will evolve as well and I think that is pretty cool. I enjoy going to concerts as well, and wish to go to some when I am older to see how much they evolved so I could tell my kids someday when I have a family of my own. Plus, songs hold memories, and that is a way I can remember funny or great past times when I hear a throwback on the radio once I get old.
